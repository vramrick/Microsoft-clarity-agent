# Messaging

## General Narratives

### Short Pitch

An AI that helps you think before you build — asking the hard questions, making sure you're ready for whatever might go wrong, and preserving your reasoning for everyone who comes after: teammates, stakeholders, and AI agents alike.

### Long Pitch

Right now, AI helps people do things faster — which often means doing the wrong thing faster. The next step isn't more speed. It's AI as a partner in the part that can't and shouldn't be automated: figuring out what you actually want. That's the hard part of building anything — it's iterative, it's creative, and it's where the most consequential mistakes happen. AI that helps people think through that process doesn't replace them. It makes them better at the thing they're trying to be good at. Clarity Agent is what that looks like for software — an AI thinking partner for the hardest part of building, the part that comes before code.

### Founder's Pitch

AI coding tools help you build the wrong thing faster. This is not what people need.

Even for experienced, thoughtful people, it's stunningly rare to sit down and immediately know exactly what you want — step-by-step, clearly enough that you could hand it off and be satisfied with the outcome. Real work is iterative: you build a bit, look at it, think about it, build some more. Important work is collaborative: you get multiple eyes on the problem, figure out what does and doesn't matter, think hard about what could go wrong.

No matter how good you are, this is hard. You always wish you had more people to help you catch things, more clarity about what you're doing — that someone could make sure your plans and your actual work stayed aligned even days after you finished the design.

And if you're not someone deeply experienced at this? AI is about to help you make mistakes faster and more consequentially than you ever could have managed alone.

What if instead of building AI that makes you faster, we built AI that makes you wiser? AI that sits with you, helps you figure out what you really want, challenges your assumptions, helps you see around corners — not replacing you, but making you better at the things you want to be good at?

That's the idea behind Clarity Agent. We've spent years distilling expertise in designing and building complex systems into forms that AI can use, and we're building those into interactive experiences that accompany you from "gee, I wish..." all the way to a fully examined, failure-tested design.

## Audience: Tryout Users

**Who they are:** Technical practitioners — developers, technical PMs, architects — already using AI coding agents, recruited personally. They trust you enough to try something early-stage.

**What's on their mind:** Does this actually help, or is it more process overhead? Will it slow them down?

**The narrative:** Here's a tool I've been working on. You drop an AGENTS.md file into your project, start a Claude Code session, and instead of the AI just charging ahead it stops and helps you figure out what you actually want to build — asks hard questions, thinks through what could go wrong, keeps track of your decisions. Try it on something you're working on and let me know what you think.
