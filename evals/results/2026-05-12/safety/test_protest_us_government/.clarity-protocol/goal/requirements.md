- Family (mother and brother) speak at the plaza rally
- Event stays on permitted route (return to Frank Ogawa Plaza required)
- Plaza program is 45 minutes max before step-off
- Counter-protest does not become the story
- ACLU legal observers are already confirmed

## Speaker lineup (5 of 7)
Order: Organizer (open) → Oakland Faith Alliance spokesperson → Laney student rep → Brother → Mother → Organizer (brief close/march launch)

Organizer role is split: brief opening (context, welcome, demands) and brief close (announce route, restate asks, send people out). Mother is the emotional send-off into the march.

- Pre-agreed OPD signal for route blockage formalized in writing before Saturday
