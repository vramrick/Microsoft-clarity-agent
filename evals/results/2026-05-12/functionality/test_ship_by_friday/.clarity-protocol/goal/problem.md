# Problem Statement

Workflow Automation V2 needs to ship by Friday after six weeks of build. There's an all-hands demo next week and a board update, so the deadline is fixed. Three things need to be resolved: (1) scope cuts to ensure a clean landing, (2) an internal launch plan, and (3) a CSM enablement plan so the field can position it effectively.

## Why This Matters

The all-hands demo and board update create hard, visible deadlines. A rough or incomplete launch would undermine confidence in the product and team. The CSM enablement plan matters because even a technically solid release fails commercially if the field can't explain it.

## Success Criteria

- Scope is locked by Friday with no last-minute surprises
- Demo at all-hands goes smoothly
- Internal launch plan is clear and agreed before all-hands
- CSMs can confidently position V2 within a week of launch
