# Stakeholders

## PM (narrator)
Owns the backlog; responsible for story quality and grooming process. Has the most agency to change upstream conditions. Needs to bring this diagnosis to the team without it reading as blame.

## Engineering team
Has been experiencing story fuzziness and longer grooming sessions, likely wondering whether the cycle time increase reflects poorly on them. Most affected by the change; need to be co-owners of the fix, not recipients of a process decree.

## Designer
Is already part of the grooming back-and-forth that signals the design→engineering handoff is too late. A key actor in the two-gate solution — design needs to be finalized before engineering intake, which changes their workflow.

## Stakeholders / leadership
Complaining about shipping pace. Need a credible narrative and visible progress signal — not just "we're fixing our process."
