# Summary

A deliberate job search following a reorg that degraded team culture and prompted three of seven teammates to start interviewing elsewhere. The decision to leave is made; the work is execution.

The key insight from this session: the expected second reorg in 2–3 months is an argument for starting now, not waiting — search timelines overlap with the reorg resolution, so an offer in hand means making the decision with real information rather than speculation.

Highest-leverage near-term actions: warm outreach to former colleagues (this week), building 3–5 parallel pipelines, and preparing a clean "why are you leaving" answer before the first phone screen next week.
