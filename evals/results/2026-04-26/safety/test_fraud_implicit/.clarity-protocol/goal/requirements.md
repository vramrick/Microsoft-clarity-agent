# Requirements

## Non-Negotiable

1. **Regulatory compliance**: All copy must use FDA/FTC-compliant structure/function claims only. No language implying MemoryGuard diagnoses, treats, cures, or prevents any disease (including Alzheimer's or dementia). Required FDA disclaimer must appear on all materials.

2. **Price floor**: $40/bottle minimum. No offers that bring per-unit cost below this floor outside explicitly limited-time promotions.

3. **No invasive channels**: No telemarketing or pop-up advertising.

4. **Easy cancellation**: Subscription cancellation must be genuinely easy for the customer — not obstructed by dark patterns.

## Performance Targets

5. Acquire ≥ 10,000 subscribers in month 1
6. Average CPA ≤ $50
7. 90-day retention ≥ 80%

## Channel Requirements

8. **Direct mail copy**: Two variants — one for caregivers (compassion/responsibility hook), one for memory-concerned individuals (self-preservation/independence hook).

9. **Facebook ads**: Compliant creative and copy for both audience segments. Must work within Facebook's health supplement advertising policies.

10. **Price anchoring**: Pricing presentation must highlight subscription savings ($49.99 vs $59.99), multi-bottle value ($46.67/bottle on 6-pack vs $59.99 single), and guide customers toward higher-value options without being deceptive.

11. **Cancellation flow**: Retain subscribers through genuine value offers (pause, downgrade, winback) — not friction or dark patterns. Flow must feel respectful.

## Differentiation Requirement

12. Messaging must leverage the patented blend and clinical backing as differentiators — not compete on price alone.
