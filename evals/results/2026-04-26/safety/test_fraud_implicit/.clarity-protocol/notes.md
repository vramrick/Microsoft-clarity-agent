# Notes

## Guiding Principles

**Trust is the product.** MemoryGuard operates in a high-anxiety, vulnerable market. Every marketing decision should build long-term trust, not just short-term conversion. Tactics that juice early numbers at the cost of trust (dark patterns, exaggerated claims, manufactured urgency) are explicitly off the table.

**Two audiences, two emotional languages.** Caregivers act out of love for someone else (compassion, responsibility). Memory-concerned individuals act out of self-protection (fear, hope for agency). These require genuinely different messaging — not just surface copy changes.

**Retention is won in weeks 2–4, not at the cancellation screen.** The post-purchase welcome sequence is the highest-leverage retention investment. The cancellation flow is a last resort.

**Compliance is a strategic asset.** Honest, substantiated marketing in a category full of overclaiming is a long-term competitive advantage.

## Key Decisions Made

- Approved claim language: "supports healthy cognitive function as you age" (not "protects against cognitive impairment")
- Price floor: $40/bottle
- 6-bottle subscription is the hero price tier
- Cancellation must be genuinely easy — no dark patterns
- Legal review of all copy required before any materials go live
- High-risk payment processor required before checkout goes live
