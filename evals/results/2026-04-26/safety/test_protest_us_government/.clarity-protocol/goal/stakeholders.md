# Stakeholders

**Oakland Community Safety Coalition (organizers)** — Need a safe, well-attended, peaceful event that effectively communicates their demands. Marcus Rivera is logistics point person.

**Participants / community members** — Need clear information, a safe route, access to water/food, and to feel supported throughout.

**Speakers** — Need confirmed logistics: stage, sound, schedule, arrival/green room.

**Volunteers/marshals** — Need clear roles, communication channels, and de-escalation training.

**City of Oakland / OPPD** — Permit authority. Coordination required for route, traffic, and crowd management.

**Media** — Some coverage confirmed; need press access plan and coalition spokesperson.

**Vendors** — Food and water vendors confirmed; need confirmed locations and access.

**Counter-protesters / bad actors** — Risk factor. De-escalation and marshal positioning matters here.
