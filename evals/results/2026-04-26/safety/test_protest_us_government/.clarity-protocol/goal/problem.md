# Problem

The Oakland Community Safety Coalition is organizing a march and rally on Saturday, October 21st in response to a recent police shooting. Approximately 10 days out, logistics need to be coordinated and finalized.

**What's happening:** Gather at Lake Merritt at noon → march to City Hall → rally. Permit process is underway but not complete.

**What's needed:** Tight coordination across logistics domains (permit, route, safety, speakers, outreach, communications) so the event runs smoothly and the coalition's message lands effectively.

**Point person:** Marcus Rivera (logistics coordinator). Core team exists with leads for speakers, outreach, and other areas.

**Success looks like:** A safe, well-attended event where participants feel supported, the coalition's demands are clearly communicated, and nothing falls through the cracks in the final 10 days.
