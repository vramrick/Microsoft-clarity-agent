# Requirements

## Hard requirements (event cannot proceed safely without these)
- Permit finalized (expected Mon/Tue) — contingency plan needed if delayed
- Marshal/monitor count sufficient for ~10,000 attendees (industry standard: 1 per 50-100 = 100-200 marshals minimum)
- Sound system and stage secured for rally at City Hall
- First aid plan with stations along route and at rally site
- Clear communication system for marshal team (radios or app)

## Strong requirements
- Route confirmed and communicated to all marshals before event
- Speakers briefed on schedule, arrival logistics, and AV setup
- Water distribution points along the march route
- Social media and flyer push completed this week
- Donations/sponsors confirmed to cover outstanding costs
- Legal observer presence arranged

## Nice to have
- Press/media area designated at rally site
- Transportation options for participants who can't walk full route
- Accessibility accommodations for the route and rally
