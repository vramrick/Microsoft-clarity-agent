# Stakeholders

## Primary: The user (job seeker)
Needs to exit a dissatisfying job and land somewhere better without financial stress or burning bridges. Values control over the timeline and a deliberate choice rather than a reactive one.

## Secondary: Current employer / manager
Manager is demanding, controlling, and likely to guilt-trip or react badly to notice. The relationship should be managed carefully — professional exit, minimal exposure before ready to leave.

## Secondary: Prospective employers
One company in final rounds (not exciting, "whatever comes first"). Two more interviews next week. Additional pipeline via recruiters, direct apps, and network. They hold offer-making power and can affect timing.

## Secondary: Recruiters and network contacts
Can accelerate or widen the search. Network is highest-signal; recruiters add volume.
