# Requirements

## Must-haves for any acceptable path forward

1. **Customer gets a credible, honest commitment.** Whatever is offered must be achievable. No sign-and-renegotiate tactics.
2. **CEO is briefed before the deal closes.** The organizational risk must be visible at the right level before a contract is signed.
3. **VP Sales and Engineering present a unified position.** No parallel or conflicting messages to the customer or CEO.
4. **The platform migration impact is explicitly weighed, not silently absorbed.** Any Q2 decision must acknowledge the 6–8 week slip and get sign-off from someone with authority over that tradeoff.

## Constraints

- Engineering lead estimates Q3 at earliest for full SSO/provisioning (native build).
- Pulling to Q2 costs 6–8 weeks of platform migration progress.
- Customer has not signed; commitment is verbal only.
- Third-party integration path (Okta/Auth0) is unscoped — cost and feasibility unknown.
- Customer's actual requirements and timeline flexibility are unconfirmed.
