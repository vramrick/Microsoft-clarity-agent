# Requirements

## Must achieve
- Reverse the velocity decline: recover toward 20 story points/sprint and cycle time toward 5 days
- Restore stakeholder confidence in delivery pace without overpromising
- Address the root cause: unmanaged architectural transition creating technical drag and team misalignment

## Must not do
- Add more ceremony/overhead to a team already adjusting to formal scrum
- Dilute team focus further (avoid adding headcount during active architecture migration)
- Mask the problem with process changes that don't address underlying causes

## Constraints
- Team is 6 people (3 BE, 2 FE, 1 QA); no change in headcount or leadership
- The microservices migration is in-flight and cannot simply be abandoned
- Stakeholder pressure is active — the intervention needs visible signals, not just internal metrics

## Key design choices
- Option 5 (manage architectural transition as a project) is highest priority
- Option 3 (reduce backlog scope) is complementary and medium priority
- Options 1, 2, 4 are low/very low priority
- The team alignment gap must be addressed as part of Option 5 — project management alone is insufficient
