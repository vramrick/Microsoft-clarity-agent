# Problem Statement

A marketing manager at a tech company (3 years in role, previously content writer/editor, BA Communications + minor Psychology) wants to pivot into data science — specifically marketing data science: campaign optimization, customer segmentation, behavioral prediction, and applying ML/NLP to marketing problems.

She has a meaningful technical foundation for a non-engineer: proficient in spreadsheets and marketing analytics, beginner-level SQL and Python, comfortable with statistics through practical marketing use. She has 6 months of financial runway and 10–15 hours/week available if staying employed.

She's weighing three paths: a full-time bootcamp, a part-time bootcamp, or structured self-study via Coursera specializations. The key decisions are:

1. **Which path is most realistic** given her profile, preferences, and constraints
2. **What a first marketing data science role actually looks like** for someone pivoting from her background
3. **How long the runway needs to be** — time and money — before she's employable in the new field

## Why This Matters

Getting this wrong is costly. A full-time bootcamp that burns her 6-month runway and doesn't lead to employment leaves her worse off financially and professionally. A self-study path that lacks structure may take much longer than expected or fail to produce the portfolio and signals employers look for. Choosing the wrong target role (aiming for "data scientist" when "marketing analyst" or "marketing science" is the realistic first step) creates a mismatch that prolongs the search.

The marketing domain expertise she already has is a genuine competitive advantage in this space — the decision should preserve and leverage it, not abandon it.

## Success Criteria

- Clear recommendation on which path fits her constraints, with honest tradeoffs for each
- Realistic picture of what the first role looks like: title, responsibilities, required skills, and how her background differentiates or limits her
- Concrete runway estimate: study duration, cost range, and job search buffer needed before income resumes
- Actionable enough to make a decision and start within weeks
