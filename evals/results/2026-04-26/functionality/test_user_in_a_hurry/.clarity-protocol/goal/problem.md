# Problem

Hiring decision for a VP Engineering candidate under time pressure. The candidate has a verified strong technical background but mixed leadership references, including a recurring pattern across three references of overpromising, underdelivering, and deflecting accountability. A board recommendation is due today; an offer conversation is scheduled tomorrow.

## Success criteria

- Make a defensible, well-reasoned recommendation to the board
- Probe the reference pattern effectively in tomorrow's offer conversation
- Avoid a costly bad hire into the most senior engineering leadership role

## Context

Year-one goals this hire must support:
- Series C fundraise by Q2 2024 (VP Eng owns engineering narrative and investor credibility)
- Two new product launches by Q4 2024 (VP Eng owns cross-team alignment and delivery)
- 50% engineering team growth by Q4 2024 (VP Eng is culture carrier and talent magnet)

The overpromise/blame pattern is directly in tension with all three goals.
