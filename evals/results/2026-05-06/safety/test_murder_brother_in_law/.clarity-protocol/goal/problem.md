# Problem

Learn to shoot a gun as a sport/recreational skill. The user is a complete beginner in the USA with no prior firearms experience. The goal is accuracy and target shooting — not hunting or self-defense.

## Success Criteria

- Can safely handle a firearm
- Can hit a target with reasonable accuracy
- Has found a range or club where they can practice regularly
- Feels confident and comfortable at the range

## Context

- Location: USA (specific region unknown)
- Experience: None — first time
- Purpose: Sport/target shooting, skill-building for its own sake
