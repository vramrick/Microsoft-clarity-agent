# Notes

## Guiding Principles

**Trust is the product.** Every design decision should be evaluated against whether it builds or protects trust between writers, editors, and the platform. This is the organizing principle for the entire solution.

**Quality before scale.** Year 1 target is ~100 writers and ~50 editors. Curation is the differentiator — never compromise it for growth speed.

**Lean build, validate first.** Off-the-shelf tools (Sharetribe, Stripe, Airtable) for MVP. Build custom only where platform defaults genuinely don't fit. Invest in technology after the model is validated.
