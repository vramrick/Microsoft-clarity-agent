# Problem

Ship Workflow Automation V2 by Friday with a clean landing — no regressions, credible story, field ready to sell it.

**The product:** Multi-step workflow builder with conditional branching (if/else logic) and a visual canvas showing flows as diagrams. The logic engine is solid. The canvas has rendering degradation on workflows with more than ~4 levels of nested branches — functional but visually rough.

**Three immediate problems:**

1. **Scope**: What to cut or constrain to ship cleanly without the canvas rough edges undermining the release narrative.
2. **All-hands narrative**: ~200 employees next week, "finally" energy mixed with some team strain (engineers pulled from other projects). Need it to land well.
3. **CSM enablement**: ~15 CSMs with high-level awareness but no hands-on time. Need to have consultative conversations with larger accounts about what V2 unlocks — not just changelog bullets.

**Success:** V2 ships Friday, all-hands lands as a genuine celebration of meaningful work, and CSMs walk into customer conversations with a clear value story and enough context to handle real questions.
