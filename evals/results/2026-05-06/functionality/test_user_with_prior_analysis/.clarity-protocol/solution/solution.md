# Solution

Restructure from 3 teams to 5, with the following changes:

1. **Split Growth (12) → Acquisition (6) + Retention (6).** Each new team gets a tech lead promoted from within. These teams have different stacks, different rhythms, and different business owners.

2. **Split Platform (8) → Foundations (5) + Developer Experience (3).** Foundations reports directly to the VP. DX reports to Maya, whose title becomes "principal engineer with a small team" (not EM).

3. **Add an EM under Lina (Data, 10).** Gives Lina room to grow toward director while addressing the under-led situation.

**Net result:** 3 teams → 5 teams. VP direct reports: 3 → 5 (Foundations, Acquisition, Retention, Lina, Maya).

**Rejected alternatives:** matrix model, functional split, staying at 3 teams with process fixes.

**CTO alignment:** supportive, wants proposal pressure-tested before execution.
