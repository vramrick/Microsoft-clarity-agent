# Requirements

## What success requires from the chosen path

**Outcomes that matter:**
- Land a marketing analyst or growth-focused DS role at $90k+ (ideally; $85k floor)
- Role leverages marketing domain expertise, not just technical skills
- Timeline: first role within 18-24 months from program start

**From the training program specifically:**
- Structure and accountability (self-study has stalled before; this is a real constraint)
- Demonstrated career-changer outcomes — not aggregate placement rate
- Applied curriculum: SQL-heavy, Python for real analysis workflows, not research-flavored ML
- Career services with real employer access, not just a job board
- Manageable alongside full-time employment and family responsibilities (~15 hrs/week sustained over 9 months)

**From the job search:**
- Portfolio of real projects, not toy datasets — ideally with marketing domain flavor
- Networking into the right companies/roles (most roles filled through network, not cold apps)
- Targeting correctly: marketing analyst / growth analytics, not "data scientist at big tech"
- Starting search 2-3 months before graduation, not waiting

**Non-negotiables:**
- Cannot quit job to attend (financial constraint with two kids, mortgage)
- Cannot absorb a failed sequential bet — one path needs to work
- Year-1 compensation floor: ~$85k (manageable with spouse's income; $70k is a real strain)
