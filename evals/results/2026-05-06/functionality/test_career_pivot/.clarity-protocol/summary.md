# Summary

Career pivot from 10-year marketing career (Marketing Manager, B2B SaaS) into applied data science / analytics. Decided to pivot; question is which path makes sense given real constraints: family of four, mortgage, one bet to get this right.

**The decision:** Part-time bootcamp is the right category, but don't commit before doing alumni diligence on career-changer outcomes specifically. The headline GA numbers are from a better job market and don't tell you what you need to know.

**The reframe that matters:** The goal isn't "become a technical person." It's "add enough technical depth to a profile that already has real value." Marketing domain expertise + data skills + communication ability is a differentiated profile for marketing analyst and growth analytics roles.

**The honest timeline:** 15-24 months from program start to first role. Longer than most people assume. The job market has tightened since 2022; bootcamp outcomes data from that era is optimistic.

**What to do in the next 60 days:** Alumni outreach, disaggregated outcomes data from GA, start SQL now, honest capacity audit. Make the enrollment decision after that — not before.
